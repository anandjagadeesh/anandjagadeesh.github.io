Anand is a freelance software engineer and an AI/ML enthusiast. He has worked as a Software Engineer in the PowerMax Replication and Cloud team at Dell EMC (Dell Technologies) where he was a part of the PowerMax Remote Replication team (also known as SRDF or RDF). Apart from helping in making the product better, he helped the BU in employee engagement, by being a part of the PowerMax University Relations team and VMAX Innovation Network. Apart from coding and volunteering activities, he enjoys being a shutterbug and is a learning photographer. His hobbies and interests include experimenting with latest technology in the fields of AI and cloud, helping students learn latest technology, conducting technical and career guidance classes for students, public speaking, mentoring young engineers and creating learning content for different technologies through his initiatives. He aspires to build an intelligent HCI system on his own that adapts an operating system to the user, someday.
