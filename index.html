About Anand

Computer Programmer, IEEE/ACM/ISOC Member, ShutterBug and Storyteller

View Professional Timeline

Anand is a Software Engineer 1 in the PowerMax Replication and Cloud at Dell EMC (Dell Technologies) where he is a part of the PowerMax Remote Replication team(Also known as SRDF or RDF). Apart from helping in making the product better, he helps the BU in employee engagement by being a part of the PowerMax University Relations team and VMAX Innovation Network. He has been part of multiple startup environments during his undergraduate course. He is volunteering as the Secretary at IEEE Computer Society Bangalore Chapter and as the Young Professionals representative for Bangalore Section at IEEE Computer Society Region 10(Asia-Pacific) SYP - India Committee. Apart from coding and volunteering activities, he enjoys being a shutterbug and is a learning photographer. His hobbies and interests include experimenting with latest technology in the fields of AI and cloud, helping students learn latest technology, conducting technical and career guidance classes for students, public speaking, mentoring young engineers and creating learning content for different technologies through his initiatives. He aspires to build an intelligent HCI system on his own that adapts an operating system to the user, someday.
